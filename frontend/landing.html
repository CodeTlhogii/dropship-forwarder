Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shipping with us

How long does shipping from China to South Africa take?

Standard shipping takes 10-15 business days from China to South Africa. Express shipping available for 5-7 days.

What is DDP and why do I need it?

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) means all customs duties and taxes are prepaid. Your customer pays nothing on delivery — higher conversion rates for you.

Do you ship from the USA as well?

Yes! We have warehouse facilities in both China and the USA. Shipping from USA to South Africa takes 7-12 days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT / wire transfer, credit card via PayFast, and wallet deposits. Monthly subscription available.
